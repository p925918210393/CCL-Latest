### The Problem
Maintaining a clean and efficient workspace is a constant challenge for power users and IT professionals. Over time, systems accumulate temporary files, redundant registry entries, and unnecessary startup items that degrade performance. Manual cleaning is often inconsistent, leading to fragmented storage, slow boot times, and potential privacy vulnerabilities through unmanaged browser traces and application logs. Without a structured toolkit, system maintenance becomes a reactive rather than a proactive process.
